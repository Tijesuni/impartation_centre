About Us

Rev. Olutunde Esan

Rev. Olutunde Joel Esan is the founder of Evidence of Great Grace Int'l Church, Impartation Centre.
He is a set man called of God to raise men and women that will practically manifest the grace of God in all areas of their life, he is sent with the message of Truth to liberate and transform mankind from the bondage of the devil and sin, with the message of Grace for impartation and he is sent to birth celebration in the lives of men through fulfillment.

Rev. (Mrs) Oluwaseun Deborah Esan

She is the wife of the minister in-charge of Evidence of Great Grace Int'l Church

OUR VISION
  • Raising men that will practically manifest the grace of God.
  • Imparting men with the grace for the heavenly race.
  • Imparting stewards with the grace to become great.
OUR MISSION

Reaching nations with the word of truth for the liberation of mankind
